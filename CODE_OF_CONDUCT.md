# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place for everyone. We pledge to make participation a harassment free experience for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Things that help:

- Being kind and respectful to other people.
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started.
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community.

Things that are not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing other people's private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and when someone is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, open a confidential report by contacting the maintainers through the repository's contact channels. Maintainers will review and respond, and may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
